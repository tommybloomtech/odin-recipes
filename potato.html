Baked Potato Recipe

What is a baked potato?

A baked potato, known in some parts of the United Kingdom (though not generally Scotland) as a jacket potato, is a preparation of potato.

Ingredients:

  • 1 russet potato
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • Pinch of fresh cracked black pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oven to 450
  2. Line baking sheet with foil
  3. Prep potato by poking holes in it with fork
  4. Bake for 25 minutes
  5. Brush outside of potato with olive oil
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  7. Bake for 20 minutes
  8. Cut potato and serve
